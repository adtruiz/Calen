# Retro Cash Rush - Multiplayer Cash Prize Game

## Overview
Weekly live 2D retro racing game where players compete for real cash prizes every Wednesday at 10 PM. Multiplayer casual gaming platform.

## Current Status: PAUSED
- **Stage**: MVP development phase
- **Last Activity**: September 2024

## Tech Stack
- **Client**: Vanilla JavaScript/Canvas
- **Server**: Python/Node
- **Multiplayer**: WebSocket real-time sync
- **Game Engine**: Custom 2D platformer physics
- **Testing**: Playwright (multi-browser)

## Game Mechanics
- Multiplayer races
- Elimination rounds
- Skill-based leagues
- Cash prize pools

## Business Model
- Entry fees: $5-10
- Winner: 50% of pool
- Top 5: 30% of pool
- House: 20% cut
